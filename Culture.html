The culture of Bangladesh is intertwined with the culture of the Bengal region of the Indian subcontinent. In Bangladesh music and dance styles generally fall under the three categories of folk,classical and modern.The country’s traditional folk songs are rich with themes such as love.Lyrics come from the nation’s culture,mysticism and spirituality.Folk songs includeBaul,Murshidi,Bhatiali and some of the lyricists include Hason Raja.
