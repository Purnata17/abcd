  • National Anthem

    • "Amar Sonar Bangla" is the national anthem of Bangladesh.An ode to Mother Bengal,the lyrics were written by Bengali Rabindranath Tagore. The song was written in 1905 during the first partition of Bengal,when the ruling British Empire had an undivided province of Bengal Presidency split into two parts.
